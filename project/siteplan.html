TradeX - Site Plan

Empowering Local Commerce Through Technology

Site Name

TradeX - This name reflects the core idea of the site: a marketplace where users can trade goods or services locally. It is short, brandable, and relevant to the mission of simplifying local buying and selling.

Site Purpose

TradeX is designed to empower individuals within communities to buy, sell, or promote freelance services without relying on expensive e-commerce platforms. The platform will include features such as product listings, service advertisements, and direct contact between buyers and sellers.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #333333 - Used for headers, nav bar, and footer background for a professional look.
  • Accent Color: #007bff - Applied to buttons and links for strong call-to-action emphasis.
  • Background: #f4f4f4 - Soft background for general readability.
  • Text Color: #000000 - Standard black for body text for maximum legibility.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - Clean, modern look for titles and headers.
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - Highly readable for content paragraphs and forms.
  • Font files will be imported via Google Fonts in the final project.

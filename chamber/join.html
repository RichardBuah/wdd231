Join the Chamber

Become a member of the Cape Coast Chamber of Commerce to enjoy networking opportunities, business resources, and community influence.

Membership Levels & Benefits

  • Non-Profit Membership: Reduced dues for charities & NGOs. Includes basic directory listing and access to training workshops.
  • Silver Membership: Standard membership for businesses. Includes directory listing, networking luncheons, and social media mentions.
  • Gold Membership: Premier level with maximum benefits. Includes prominent directory listing, event sponsorship opportunities, advertising in chamber publications, and all Silver perks.

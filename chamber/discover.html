Discover Cape Coast

Learn more about our vibrant coastal town – from its rich history to the thriving community of today.

Our History

Cape Coast was founded in 1850 as a small fishing village and has grown into a bustling coastal city. Over the decades, it developed a strong maritime industry and a rich cultural heritage. The historic downtown area still features 19th-century architecture alongside modern businesses.

Demographics

  • Population: 45,000 (2025)
  • Founded: 1850
  • Key Industries: Tourism, Maritime Trade, Technology
  • Annual Visitors: ~500,000
